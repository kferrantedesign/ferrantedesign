Flatpack Chair

This project aimed to create flatpack chair that must be made from 1/2-inch thick unfinished OSB, using no more than a 29” x 47” sheet. It must fit into a 1-inch thick package, be easy to assemble, and support a 250-pound live load.

Prototype Tools: Laser Cutter, Waterjet, and Woodshop tools

Softwares: Solidworks, Fusion 360, Keyshot

Year: 2022
