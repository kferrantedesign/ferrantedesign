Rhythm Train

This project aimed to create a toy that could teach children rhythm through visual pieces and sounds. Designed with the brand Kiwi Co in mind. The company designs science and art project for kids to learn about the STEM field.

Prototype Tools: 3D Printing, CNC Machine, and Woodshop tools

Softwares: Solidworks, Fusion 360, Keyshot, Adobe Photoshop

Year: 2023
